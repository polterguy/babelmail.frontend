BabelMail

This application was automatically generated with Magic, and was based upon the generator-ngx-rocket Angular starter-kit. The purpose of the app is to serve as an enterprise email inbox/outbox, to for instance have a common email address where customers and clients can request support, send documents, etc. The application is not intended to serve individual email requirements for employees, but rather to provide a common email inbox for multiple employees, allowing your employees to answer emails sent to common support email adress, etc.

The backend can also easily be integrated into any CRM system you wish to integrate it with, since it has a rich web API, allowing you to associate emails with for instance customers and clients of your company. It also allows you to easily create semi-automated email functions, such as providing an email address for your CRM system(s), and semi-automate processes occurring as your CRM system receives an email from a client or an employee. The system stores all sent and received emails in an SQL database, which again has a rich web API, allowing you to easily query the system, and/or associate emails with customers and clients within any other types of systems, that can be easily integrated with BabelMail using its web API. The system also supports templates, which are pre-defined email templates, that can easily be braided in with customer specific data, allowing you to send emails based upon templates.
